Get a BootStack managed cloud in three easy steps

  1. 1 We build your cloud

    Together, we design your cloud to the required size and specifications — on your site, in your datacentre or hosted with one of our partners.

  2. 2 We operate your cloud

    Our OpenStack experts take full responsibility for the management, monitoring and 24x7 availability of your new cloud. The hardware and software are covered by proactive maintenance too.

  3. 3 We transfer control

    If you decide to take the cloud in-house, we will train your team on its operations to ensure a smooth handover.

BootStack can be customised to your requirements

Hosted BootStack

We have chosen QTS and eShelter as a hosted option for BootStack worldwide, providing the highest performing dedicated cloud infrastructure available. Their customisation options enable OpenStack to be used for provisioning bare metal as well as virtual machines from datacentres around the world.

Read the QTS press release

BootStack Direct

Whereas a Hosted BootStack leaves the operations to us, BootStack Direct guarantees the operation of the cloud goes directly to you, including training and support for your staff. We will get your OpenStack cloud up and running in days, preparing your team to become OpenStack operations experts.
