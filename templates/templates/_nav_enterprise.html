Containers

Dramatically improve VM density with LXD containers for traditional apps, and build new cloud-native apps with Kubernetes on Ubuntu, for portability across Google and Azure cloud kubernetes services. Ubuntu is the top OS for Docker deployment and ops.

Private cloud

For private clouds we offer fully managed Openstack as a cost-effective VMware replacement. Ubuntu is the top platform for OpenStack in large-scale deployments.
