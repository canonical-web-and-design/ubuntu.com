Ubuntu Advantage for Infrastructure (UA-I)

Ubuntu Advantage for Infrastructure is a comprehensive subscription to Canonical technology and expertise, helping organisations around the world get the most out of their Ubuntu deployments. It includes access to:

  • Extended Security Maintenance (ESM) provides critical security updates
  • Landscape system management simplifies use of Ubuntu at scale
  • Livepatch enables automatic kernel security hotfixes without rebooting
  • Knowledge Base offers a private archive of expert-written articles and tutorials
  • Phone and web-based support at multiple service levels
  • FIPS certified modules to meet Common Criteria compliance
  • Ceph and Swift storage, backup and recovery capabilities

MAAS

MAAS is open source data centre automation provisioning, DNS, DHCP, and IPAM software from Canonical. Many enterprises depend on MAAS to automate and optimise provisioning for server clusters. Canonical offers Standard and Advanced support plans priced per-machine per-month or, for large volume and public clouds with dynamic scaling, we offer per-region pricing with flexible node ranges.
