Welcome to Ubuntu Advantage support

Thank you and welcome to the Ubuntu Advantage support subscription. We ask that you follow the below steps to get started.

If you have any questions please do not hesitate to contact the Canonical team through the support portal, or if having issues setting up an account, at support@canonical.com.

  1. 1 Read the service description

    Read and bookmark the service description of your subscription, as it will be a helpful reference of the scope and coverage of your support service.

  2. 2 Set up your account

    Create an Ubuntu One account in order to access Canonical's online support portal and Landscape systems management tool. To create an account:

    • Visit https://login.ubuntu.com
    • Use your current, full business email address for your login (e.g. yourname@yourcompany.com)

    If you already have an Ubuntu One account, please verify your preferred email address by replying to your subscription welcome email from support@canonical.com.

    If you have any issues or questions creating your Ubuntu One account, please visit http://ubunt.eu/create-sso or email support@canonical.com.

  3. 3 Access the support portal

    Once your Ubuntu One account is set up, you can access the customer support portal by visiting https://support.canonical.com. It is recommended to bookmark this link to simplify your access to the portal.

    Within the Ubuntu Advantage support portal, you can access the following features and resources:

    • Contact form to submit cases, easily accessible from the portal home page and ‘Contact Us’ navigation button
    • Case status page to review and update all open and/or closed cases
    • Landscape access, Canonical’s single-interface management tool
    • Extensive Knowledge Base, with technical reference articles and answers
