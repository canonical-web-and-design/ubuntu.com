The Ubuntu Advantage

Open Source support for the enterprise

Ubuntu Advantage for your infrastructure offers a single, per-node packaging of the most comprehensive software, security and IaaS support in the industry. With OpenStack and Kubernetes support included, Ubuntu Advantage for Infrastructure (UA-I) provides everything you need to future-proof your data centre.

Including Canonical Livepatch, Landscape and Extended Security Maintenance (ESM) to address security and compliance concerns, Ubuntu Advantage for Infrastructure provides a comprehensive single pane of glass for systems management, audit reporting and live kernel patching to prevent unplanned maintenance and downtime.

Infrastructure solutions

Systems management and security at scale

Automate day-to-day management with Landscape, the most cost-effective tool to support and monitor large and rapidly expanding networks of clouds, servers and desktops. Use Landscape for audit reporting, security compliance and patch management at scale. Landscape is available as a service from the cloud or on-premises for compliance with stricter security requirements.

Take control of your infrastructure with:

  • Automate updates and manage physical, virtual and cloud-based systems from a single interface
  • Monitor and managed machines at scale
  • Maintain security and compliance
  • Controlled inventory
  • Integrate with current systems
  • Install, upgrade or downgrade packages
  • Secure your infrastructure with Role-Based Access Control
  • Reduce downtime, maintain Ubuntu LTS security and compliance with the Canonical Livepatch Service

Critical kernel patches without rebooting

Available with an Ubuntu Advantage for Infrastructure subscription, the Canonical Livepatch Service enables you to apply critical kernel security fixes to your Ubuntu systems without rebooting. Available on LTS-based systems, Livepatch reduces downtime while maintaining compliance and security.
