What are Microcertifications?

Earn your qualification one topic at a time, at your own pace, in any order.

The CUBE curriculum is perfectly portoned to accomodate your busy schedule. It consists of 15 separate microcertifications, each covering a specific subject area, that can be taken in any order and at any time.

Complete CUBE Syllabus - At a Glance

  • Architecture

    Understand how the sustem is built, from top to bottom.

  • Packages

    Control and customize installations.

  • Commands

    Direct system actions and complete important tasks.

  • Devices & files

    Configure files and where they are stored.

  • bash

    Automate tasks with scripts.

  • Admin

    Master useful tasks for working with shared and individual systems.

  • Services

    Fine-tune systems for optimal performance.

  • Networking

    Link machines to perform advanced tasks.

  • Security

    Protect systems and servers from unwelcome intrusions.

  • Kernel

    Manage the heart of your Ubuntu system.

  • Storage

    Archive and retrieve data safely and securely.

  • Virtualisation

    Create, configure, and use virtual machines.

  • MicroK8s

    Streamline your Kubernetes usage with Canonical’s efficient deployment solution.

  • MAAS

    Turn data centers into bare-metal clouds using Canonical’s server provisioning technology.

  • Juju

    Keep complexity low and productivity high with Canonical’s universal operator lifecycle manager.
