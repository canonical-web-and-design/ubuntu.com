Managed Services privacy

At Canonical, we consider your privacy to be extremely important to us. Please see our privacy policy which sets out Canonical's fundamental principles on privacy and details about how Canonical collects data and what we do with it.

In relation to BootStack and Managed Kubernetes, we are aware that privacy is also especially important to you and we therefore want to reassure you that:

  • Canonical does not have direct access to the data on your OpenStack cloud or Kubernetes cluster
  • Canonical will not access the data on your environment at any time
  • Backup and recovery of the data on your environment is your responsibility
