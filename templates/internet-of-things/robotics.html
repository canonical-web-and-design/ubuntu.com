The smartest path to production grade

When prototyping robots in the lab, you need speed and flexibility. Taking your creation to production, stability and automated maintenance are key. Get all of the above with Ubuntu.

  1. 1 Get Ubuntu

    Ubuntu Server is the OS behind ROS, many of the world’s most popular drones, and most autonomous vehicle projects. It’s at least as flexible as whatever you’re using now, with a larger ecosystem and community behind it. From first install through the entire product lifecycle, our stability and developer friendliness is legendary.

  2. 2 Snap your apps

    Snaps are the gateway to vastly simplified package and application management. Go at your own pace by snapping your apps on top of Ubuntu Server first. Once you’ve seen the benefits first hand, you’ll be rushing to the next step. You’ll see!

  3. 3 Ubuntu Core

    The stability, security, and auditability of Ubuntu Core delivers will delight your enterprise customers. Take your next-gen robot to production on the only Linux OS redesigned from scratch to bring all the advantages of snaps to the robotics world.

Advantages of snaps

  • Designed for production

    Whether you’re pushing our critical security updates or launching new features, snaps automate the process. Put your robot’s software management on rails.

  • Compatible with ROS

    Snaps are compatible with ROS1 and ROS2. Refer to links below for getting started with catkin and colcon respectively.

  • Transactional

    Failed updates roll back to the last working version automatically, allowing for rapid deployment.

  • Auditable

    All snaps are digitally signed and can be verified at any time.

    On Ubuntu Core, the entire device is immutable and traceable

  • Update control

    Schedule snap updates to align with planned maintenance windows.

  • Secured

    Every application is confined to its own area for heightened security. On Ubuntu Core, the OS is locked down too.
